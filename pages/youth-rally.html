1st Annual Blessed Hope Youth Rally

Against the Current

“Love not the world, neither the things that are in the world. If any man love the world, the love of the Father is not in him. For all that is in the world, the lust of the flesh, and the lust of the eyes, and the pride of life, is not of the Father, but is of the world.”

1 John 2:15–16

The world moves in one direction. The Word of God calls us to move in another. This November, Blessed Hope Baptist Church is hosting its first annual Youth Rally for young people who want to stand for something real in a world that is drifting further from the truth every day.

For just $10, the night includes preaching from three men of God, games, food and drinks, door prizes, and gift cards. The night closes out with kickball, big volleyball, and dodgeball. Come ready to hear from the Lord and have a great time.

Preachers

Andrew Rardin, Matt White, and Nick Russo will be preaching throughout the evening. Three men committed to the Word of God and to the next generation.

Food

Food and drinks will be provided throughout the evening. Come hungry.

Activities

The night closes out with kickball, big volleyball, and dodgeball. Come ready to move.

Prizes

Gift cards and door prizes throughout the evening. Every $10 ticket gets you in the door and in the running.
