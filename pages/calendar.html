Take the Calendar With You

Add our ministry calendar directly to your phone so every event stays automatically updated.

Add to iPhone

  1. Copy the iCal link from the Ministry Calendars section below.
  2. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  3. Tap Calendar → Accounts → Add Account → Other.
  4. Tap Add Subscribed Calendar.
  5. Paste the iCal link into the Server field and tap Next.
  6. Tap Save the calendar will stay automatically updated.

Add to Android

  1. Copy the iCal link from the Ministry Calendars section below.
  2. On your phone, open a browser and go to calendar.google.com.
  3. Click the + next to "Other calendars" → From URL.
  4. Paste the iCal link into the URL field.
  5. Click Add Calendar it will sync to your Google Calendar app automatically.
