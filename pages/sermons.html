Sermon Recordings Coming This Fall

We are working to make sermon recordings available here on the website. Our goal is to have a full library online by fall 2026, including the ability to browse by series and search by Scripture passage.

Every Sunday, Pastor Josh Calabrese opens the Word of God and preaches it without apology. We believe faithful preaching is the primary means God uses to build His church, and we want those sermons accessible to anyone who wants to hear them.
