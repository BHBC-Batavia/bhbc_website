Where Will You Finish?

Every child is on a journey. Every choice leads somewhere. This summer at Blessed Hope Baptist Church, kids Pre-K through 6th Grade will lace up their shoes and join the Rocky Mountain Trail Race as we follow two runners, Chase Sidetracks and Miles Ready, through the mountains of Colorado. Along the way they will discover that every step matters, every direction counts, and the only one who can truly guide you on the trail is Jesus Christ.

Through games, crafts, music, and Bible teaching, children will be challenged to run their race for the Lord. The goal of life is not simply to start. The goal is to finish well by faithfully following the Master Trailblazer, Jesus Christ.

“I have fought a good fight, I have finished my course, I have kept the faith.”

2 Timothy 4:7

Who Can Come

VBS is open to all children Pre-K through 6th Grade. You do not have to attend Blessed Hope or any church to come. All are welcome. Grab your map, lace up your shoes, and bring a friend.

Good to Know

VBS is completely free. No registration fee, no cost for materials. Dress comfortably and be ready to move. There will be games, activities, and a lot of fun on the trail.

Need a Ride?

Blessed Hope has a 15-passenger van and we are happy to provide transportation for children who need a ride to VBS. Reach out to us ahead of time and we will make it work.
