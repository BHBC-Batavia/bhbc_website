First Time Here?

You Are Welcome Here.

We know that walking into a church for the first time can feel intimidating especially if you have never been to a Baptist church before. We want to make it as easy as possible. There is no dress code, no pressure, and no expectation. Just come. We are a family and we are glad to make room for you.

Who We Are

We are a small, friendly, independent Baptist church. You will find real people, genuine community, and the Word of God preached faithfully every week. We are not a big production we are a family.

Our Story

No Surprises

What to Expect

We want your first Sunday to feel comfortable. Here is a simple walkthrough of what you can expect from the moment you pull into the parking lot.

01

Parking

We have a large parking lot right in front of the building on Galloway Road. Plenty of space pull right in and someone will likely greet you before you even reach the door.

02

When You Walk In

You will be greeted at the door. We are a friendly church and people genuinely want to meet you. Do not be surprised if someone shakes your hand and learns your name that is just who we are.

03

Sunday School at 9:30 AM

If you arrive at 9:30 AM you will be directed to the Sunday School class that fits your age group. We have classes for every stage of life from toddlers all the way to adults. It is a great way to get to know people before the main service.

04

The Worship Service at 10:30 AM

The morning service typically runs about an hour and fifteen minutes. We sing hymns from a hymnal, have a time of prayer, and then Pastor Josh preaches from the King James Bible. It is a traditional Baptist service reverent, warm, and centered on the Word of God.

05

Children

Children are welcome in the service. We also have Sunday School classes specifically for children where they will be taught the Bible in an age-appropriate way by caring, faithful teachers.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do I have to be a member to attend?

Not at all. Everyone is welcome to attend any of our services. Membership is something we talk about after you have been coming for a while and feel at home with us.

What Bible translation do you use?

We stand on the King James Bible. It is the translation we preach from, teach from, and use in all of our services. If you do not have one, we have Bibles available and you are welcome to follow along on your phone.

Is there anything for my kids?

Yes. Our Sunday School program has classes for every age starting at two years old. Children are also welcome to sit with you in the main service.

How long is the service?

The worship service service is typically about an hour and fifteen minutes. Sunday School runs for one hour before the service.

What if I have more questions?

Call us at (585) 201-7467 or email info@blessedhopebaptist.church and someone will be happy to help. You can also reach Pastor Josh directly at pastor@blessedhopebaptist.church — he genuinely wants to hear from you.
