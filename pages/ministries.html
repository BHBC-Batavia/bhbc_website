Ministries

Places to belong, grow, and serve at every stage of life.

Get Involved

There Is a Place for You Here

Whether you are two years old or eighty-two, there is a place for you at Blessed Hope. Our Sunday School classes meet every Sunday morning at 9:30 AM before the worship service, and our community outreach goes out once a month to love our neighbors in Genesee County.

Every Sunday at 9:30 AM

Sunday School

Our Sunday School program offers age-appropriate Bible instruction for every member of your family. Classes run every Sunday morning at 9:30 AM, one hour before the worship service service.

Ages 2 – Pre-K

Bright Beginnings

Our youngest learners are introduced to the stories, characters, and truths of Scripture in a loving, nurturing environment. Through simple lessons, songs, and activities, we lay the foundation of faith for little hearts.

1st – 6th Grade

Truth Chasers

Elementary-age students dig into the Word of God with lessons designed to help them understand who God is, what He has done, and how the Bible applies to their everyday lives. We believe children can grasp real Bible truth, and we teach them accordingly.

7th – 12th Grade

Next Up

Teenagers face real questions and real pressures. Next Up is a place where young people can study the Scripture seriously, ask hard questions, and grow in their faith alongside others who are walking the same road. We are committed to raising the next generation of faithful believers.

College – Adulthood

Faith in Focus

Our adult class is where believers of all ages come together to study the Word, encourage one another, and grow in practical Christian living. Whether you are a new believer or have walked with the Lord for decades, you will find a home in Faith in Focus.

Once a Month

Community Outreach

Every month Blessed Hope goes into the Batavia community to share the love of Christ in practical ways. From door-to-door gospel work to serving our neighbors in need, we believe the church exists not just within its four walls but in the streets and homes of our city.

Keep an eye on our calendar for the next outreach date all are welcome to join us.

Serving Our Neighbors

Meeting practical needs in our community as an expression of the love of Christ.
