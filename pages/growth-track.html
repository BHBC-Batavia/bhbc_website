Growth Track

New here? Here is how to get connected and start growing.

Getting Started

We Are Glad You Are Here.

Whether you visited for the first time last Sunday or you have been coming for a while and are wondering what is next, the Growth Track is designed to help you take the next step. It is not a program it is a pathway to belonging, growing, and serving at Blessed Hope Baptist Church.

Step 01

Gather

Join us for a Sunday service.

Everything starts by showing up. Join us every Sunday morning at 10:30 AM at 3515 Galloway Rd in Batavia. Come early at 9:30 AM for Sunday School and stay for the worship service service. You will hear the Word of God preached, worship with the congregation, and begin to meet the people of Blessed Hope.

Sunday School 9:30 AM
Worship Service 10:30 AM
Evening Service 5:30 PM
Wednesday Bible Study 7:00 PM
Plan Your Visit
Step 02

Grow

Get into the Word.

Real growth happens through regular exposure to the Bible. We want to help you develop a consistent walk with the Lord through Sunday School, Wednesday night Bible Study, and personal discipleship. Find the class that fits your stage of life and start digging into the Word with people who will walk alongside you.

Bright Beginnings Ages 2 – Pre-K
Truth Chasers 1st – 5th Grade
Next Up 7th – 12th Grade
Faith in Focus College – Adulthood
View All Ministries
Step 03

Connect

Get to know our church family.

The church is not a building or a service it is a family. We want you to know people and be known. Introduce yourself to someone new, stay after a service, come to a church fellowship event, or reach out to our pastor directly. We believe relationships are where real discipleship happens, and we want to make room for you in ours.

Get in Touch
Step 04

Belong

Become a member of Blessed Hope.

Membership at Blessed Hope is not a formality it is a commitment. It means you have trusted Christ as your Savior, followed Him in believer's baptism by immersion, and have agreed to walk together with this local church in love, accountability, and service. If you are ready to take that step, reach out to our pastor and we will walk through it with you.

Talk to Our Pastor
Step 05

Serve

Give back to the Lord and to the church.

Every member of the body has a role to play. Whether it is helping with audio and visual, assisting in Sunday School, joining our monthly community outreach, or serving in another capacity, there is a place for you to use your gifts at Blessed Hope. We would love to help you find where you fit.

Get Involved

Ready to take the first step?

Come join us this Sunday. We would love to meet you.
